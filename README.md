# 🌧️ Forks: Your Destiny

A Twilight-inspired visual novel where *you* decide — Edward or Jacob?
Built entirely with vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. No frameworks, no libraries.

🔗 **[Click Here To Play Now](https://kkaytech.github.io/twilight-vn/)**

## About

Set in the rainy town of Forks, Washington, this interactive story lets you
explore branching paths, uncover supernatural secrets, and choose your ending.
Your choices influence affinity meters for both Edward and Jacob in real time,
leading to three unique endings.

## Features

- 🎭 **25+ branching scenes** with multiple story paths
- 💜 **Live affinity meter** tracking your Edward vs Jacob choices
- ✍️ **Typewriter dialogue** with spacebar skip
- 🎵 **Background music** with toggle control
- 🌙 **3 unique endings** — Edward, Jacob, or go it alone
- 📱 Fully playable in the browser, no install needed

## How to Play

1. Click **Begin Your Story**
2. Read the dialogue — press **SPACE** to skip typing
3. Click your choices to shape the story
4. Watch the affinity meter shift as you decide
5. Reach one of three endings and replay to discover the rest

## Built With

`HTML` · `CSS` · `Vanilla JavaScript` · `Web Audio API`

> No frameworks. No dependencies. Just pure front-end storytelling.

## Run Locally

```bash
git clone https://github.com/kkaytech/forks-your-destiny
```
